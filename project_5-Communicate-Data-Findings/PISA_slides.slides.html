PISA 2012¶

by Jean-Pierre Distler¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation i wanted to have a look if the gender of a student has an impact at the score a student received in the PISA survey.

Dataset Overview¶

The original dataset contained 485490 students and 634 features. After cleaning i shortened the features to 10. Country, StudentId, InternationlGrade, BirthMonth, BirthYear, Gender, Age, MathScore, ReadingScore and ScienceScore. The scores have a range from 0 to 1000 Points.

Overall Score Distributions¶

The scores are normally distributed with a mean around 500 Points. The lowest scores in each discipline are below 20 Points and the highest are over 900.
